## Overview

Tailwind Builder AI is a free tool that leverages artificial intelligence to instantly generate custom forms , tables and charts compatible with the shadcn/ui ecosystem. Users can simply describe the component they need in plain text to get ready-to-use code.

## Features

- **AI-Powered Generation** – Quickly convert text descriptions into functional shadcn/ui code.
- **Free to Use** – Available at no cost for developers.
- **Copy-Paste Output** – Generates forms , tables and charts that follow the shadcn philosophy, ensuring easy integration and full code control.
- **Customizable** – Output is fully customizable upon pasting into your project.
